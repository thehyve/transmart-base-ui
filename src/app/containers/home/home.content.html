To load studies, connect to an API which can be added in the Settings
  1. Select and endpoint from the defaults or enter its URL and choose a name for it.
  2. Request a token. You will be redirected to a login page for the chosen endpoint. Once the token is obtained, paste in the designated area.
  3. Click save.
  1. To browse studies, click on the folder icon next to one.
  2. Child concepts can then be dragged into the cohort selection tab.
